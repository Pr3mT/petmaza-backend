## 🎯 **IMPLEMENTATION TIPS**

### **Position Guide:**
- **`top`**: Hero banners on homepage (high visibility)
- **`popup`**: Urgent offers, Prime membership, big sales (use sparingly!)
- **`sidebar`**: Related products, subscription offers (less intrusive)
- **`bottom`**: Footer offers, app downloads, referrals

### **Display Order Priority:**
1-5: Critical (Prime, New User offers)
6-10: Category sales (rotating)
11-15: Features & bundles

### **Image Dimensions Recommended:**
- Top Banner: 1920x400px
- Popup: 800x600px
- Sidebar: 400x600px
- Bottom: 1920x200px

### **Design Guidelines:**
✅ Use high-quality pet images
✅ Bold, readable fonts
✅ Clear CTA buttons
✅ Brand colors (match Petmaza theme)
✅ Mobile-responsive designs
